About MyDailyGains

MyDailyGains is a privacy-focused fitness tracking app that generates randomized daily challenges to keep your workout routine fresh and exciting.

Key Features

  • Randomized Daily Challenges - Never do the same workout twice
  • Progress Tracking - Visual calendar and streak counter
  • Fully Customizable - Choose exercises, set ranges, schedule days
  • Privacy First - All data stored locally, no accounts required

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add progress to an exercise?

Tap the + button on any exercise card to open the quick add sheet. Select an increment or enter a custom value.

Can I customize the exercises?

Yes! Go to the Settings tab to enable/disable exercises, adjust min/max values, and set which days they appear.

How do streaks work?

Complete all your daily exercises to maintain your streak. The streak counter appears in the top right of the Today tab.

Is my data backed up?

If you have iCloud enabled, your data automatically syncs across your Apple devices. All data is stored locally with no external servers.

How do I reset my progress?

In Settings, scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete All Data & Start Over". This cannot be undone.
