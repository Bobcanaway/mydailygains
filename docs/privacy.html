Overview

MyDailyGains does NOT collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.

The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any external servers.

Data Collection

MyDailyGains does not collect any personal data. We believe your fitness journey is yours alone.

Local Data Storage

All of your workout data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. This includes:

  • Daily challenges and targets
  • Exercise configurations
  • Progress tracking
  • Completion history
  • Streak information
  • Settings and preferences

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud:

  • Your workout data may sync across your Apple devices using your personal iCloud account
  • This data is encrypted and transmitted through Apple's secure infrastructure
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • iCloud is managed entirely by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policies
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings

Learn more about Apple's privacy practices: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

Push Notifications

MyDailyGains may send you local notifications (reminders) to help you stay on track with your fitness goals. These notifications:

  • Are generated locally on your device
  • Do not transmit any data externally
  • Can be disabled in iOS Settings at any time

Third-Party Services

MyDailyGains does NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social media integrations
  • Any third-party tracking tools

Children's Privacy

MyDailyGains does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages (rated 4+).

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data security is managed by iOS and your device's security features
  • We recommend keeping your device password-protected
  • Enable iCloud backup to prevent data loss if you lose your device

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Delete: You can delete all data at any time through Settings → Delete All Data
  • Export: All data is stored in standard iOS formats on your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or MyDailyGains, please contact us at:

Email: support@mydailygains.app
